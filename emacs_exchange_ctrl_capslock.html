1 交换右ctrl与Caps Lock

1.1 linux

在home下新建.xmodmap文件 

!
! Swap Caps_Lock and Control_R
!
remove Lock = Caps_Lock
remove Control = Control_R
keysym Control_R = Caps_Lock
keysym Caps_Lock = Control_R
add Lock = Caps_Lock
add Control = Control_R

1.2 windows

要想在w2000或者XP中实现这个互换，需要修改注册表,找到： HKEYLOCALMACHINE\SYSTEM\CurrentControlSet\Control\Keyboard Layout 点击 KeyboardLayout 项，使之获得焦点。再新建一个二进制值，命名为"Scancode Map" ，它的类型应该显示为 REGBINARY。

然后选择这个新建的"Scancode Map"值项，选择修改二进制值，在二 进制编辑对话框中，输入下数据： 

0000: 00 00 00 00 00 00 00 00
0008: 03 00 00 00 3A 00 1D E0
0010: 1D E0 3A 00 00 00 00 00
